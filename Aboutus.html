Aboutus

Shrinathji Electricals is basically a technical services provider established by Mr.Himanshu Desai, - Graduate in Electrical Engineering, having more than 15 years of experience of erection, operation and maintenance of 66 KV to 400 KV voltage level sub stations and lines, we are experienced team of ambitious, vibrant, young professionals having trend to update with latest developments & requirements of client and system form a core group. We believe in continual improvement of system and method and to give best techno commercial solution to all parameters.

Our human resources department is comprise of more than 7 bright electrical engineers having best managerial skill in addition to technical competency more than 9 Diploma holders with help of more than 10 Electricians are providing no complain services to our clients.

Our work contractors have an army of nearly 20 persons for erection and civil work. Our site is managed by a software support to keep record of day to day activity. All required testing instruments are available with valid calibration certificate.

We deliver complete solution on EPC -turnkey bases for EHV switch yards and HT Network and including consultation, Liaison, Equipment erectionwith civil and fabrication work, Installation,& commissioning& testing

We also undertake complete work for EHT/ HT tower Line,underground cable networkfor transmission and distribution and erection of Industrial installations on turnkey bases also Solar project, Wind Farms, , Furnace, Rolling mills and Spinning mill, are our area of expertise. We are successfully executing Operation and maintenance contract for Sub Stations and Lines. We provide complete solution to any problem related with electric installation.
